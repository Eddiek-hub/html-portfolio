My Top 3 Movies

For The Year 2024

Strange Darling

It is a psychological thriller that dives into the complex and chilling dynamics between a hunter and their prey. Directed with precision,it explores the themes of obsession, manipulation, and survival through an unpredictable narrative.The film is marked by its intense performances and a constantly shifting perspective that keeps the audience on edge, questioning who holds the upper hand. It’s a gripping watch for those who enjoy dark, cerebral storytelling.

Alien Romulus

It is a highly anticipated installment in the iconic *Alien* franchise,offering a fresh take on the legendary sci-fi horror series. Directed by Fede Álvarez, the film promises to deliver a spine-chilling blend of suspense, terror, and visceral action as it delves into humanity's encounter with the relentless Xenomorphs. With its atmospheric tension and gripping storytelling, Alien Romulus aims to honor the franchise's legacy while introducing new layers to the enduring battle for survival against one of cinema's most fearsome creatures.

The Creator

This a visually stunning sci-fi epic that explores the clash between humanity and advanced artificial intelligence in a post-apocalyptic future. Directed by Gareth Edwards, the film follows a gripping tale of love, loss, and redemption as a former soldier is tasked with hunting down the elusive "Creator" responsible for a mysterious AI weapon. With breathtaking visuals, thought-provoking themes, and emotional depth, The Creator raises compelling questions about morality, identity, and the boundaries of technology. It's a must-watch for fans of ambitious, world-building cinema.
